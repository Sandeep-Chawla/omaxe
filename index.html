A Modern Transformation Awaits, Inspired by the Success of Omaxe Chandni Chowk

Ghaziabad's historic Old Bus Stand is set for a remarkable transformation, taking inspiration from the highly successful Omaxe Chandni Chowk project. Omaxe Group, a leading Indian real estate developer, is joining hands with UPSRTC in a Public-Private Partnership (PPP) model to develop a futuristic commercial complex on the site. This project promises to be a game-changer for Ghaziabad's commercial landscape, replicating the winning formula established at Omaxe Chandni Chowk.

Blending Heritage with Modernity

The old bus stand has been a vital part of Ghaziabad's transportation network for decades. Omaxe acknowledges this heritage and aims to seamlessly blend it with modern advancements, similar to the approach taken at Omaxe Chandni Chowk. The project will be divided into two distinct sections:

  • A contemporary commercial complex: This section will house retail stores, offices, restaurants, and a multiplex, creating a vibrant hub for businesses and entertainment, mirroring the successful model of Omaxe Chandni Chowk.
  • A futuristic bus stand: The revamped bus stand will integrate smart technologies and modern infrastructure to offer a seamless travel experience.

About the Builder
